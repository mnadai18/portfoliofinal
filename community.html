Community Corps

Gaining experience through community service projects.

About

Community Corps is a part of the University of Michigan School of Information's Engaged Learning Office. This elective connects students with community service projects that help enchance their skills along with helping the community.

Requirements

Even though this elective is part of the School of Information, it is open to all University of Michigan students, because the projects can be very interdisciplined. Topics can include things like data analytics, user experience, or web design. Students don't have to have any prior experience in order to apply-- just a passion to learn and help with a social justice project.

Specifics

For my project, I started work on it during October of 2021 and will continue until around Feburary 2022. My project is confidential, but does involve my group working to create a blueprint for a company to utilize in order to optimize their website design to meet their goals. This project is a great opportunity to practice my skills in user experience, human-computer interaction, user research, and working with stakeholders.
